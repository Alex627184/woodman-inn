About Us

A True Yorkshire Local

Nestled in the heart of Clayton West, The Woodman Inn is everything a proper village pub should be — welcoming, warm and full of character. Our traditional stone-built pub has been serving the local community for years with great drinks, lively entertainment and a friendly atmosphere.

Whether you're popping in after work for a quiet pint, watching the big match live on Sky Sports, or joining us for one of our legendary Karaoke Nights — you'll always find a smile and a seat at The Woodman.

We're also happy to host your private events and celebrations — birthdays, anniversaries and get-togethers. Get in touch to discuss your requirements.
